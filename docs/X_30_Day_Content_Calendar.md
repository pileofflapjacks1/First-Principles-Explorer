# Grok Fairness Layer — 30-Day X Content Calendar

**Goal:** Showcase that I can use Grok + AI agents to build a meaningful, real-world product that solves a painful problem in the trading card community.

**Primary Audience:** TCG buyers, sellers, collectors, and small shop owners.
**Secondary Audience:** Founders, builders, and AI-curious people.

**Content Style:** Screenshots + short video clips from the live demo (https://grok-fairness-layer.replit.app). Mix of educational, entertaining, and builder-focused posts.

**Overall Narrative Arc:**
- Week 1: Introduce the problem + the project
- Week 2: Show the "magic" (inference + demo)
- Week 3: Deep dives + community angles
- Week 4: Builder story + learnings + forward-looking

**Posting Frequency:** 4–5 posts per week (mix of single posts and threads). Not every day — quality over quantity.

**Best Performing Formats on X for this project:**
- Threads (especially contrast + explanation)
- Short video clips (15–45 seconds)
- Annotated screenshots / carousels
- Polls asking the TCG community questions
